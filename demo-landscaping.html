Our story

Family owned, neighbor trusted,
Chicago proud.

M&G was started by our family right here in Chicago — two brothers with a truck, a passion for the outdoors, and a commitment to doing honest work at a fair price. Over 15 years later, we're still family-owned and operated, and we still show up the same way we did on day one.

We know our customers by name. We show up on time, clean up after ourselves, and treat every yard like it's our own. Whether it's a small front lawn or a full backyard transformation, you'll always deal directly with us — not a call center, not a subcontractor.

Our reputation is everything to us. When we finish a job, we want you to be proud to show it off to your neighbors — and we want those neighbors to call us next.

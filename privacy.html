Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Bow Lane Leads ("we", "us", or "our") operates bowlaneleads.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when you contact us through our website.

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or contact form on our website, we collect the following:

  • Your name
  • Business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Industry and service details
  • Any additional notes you provide

We do not collect any information beyond what you voluntarily submit through our contact form.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide a package recommendation
  • Follow up regarding our services

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not add you to any mailing lists without your consent.

How We Store Your Information

Form submissions are logged in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Bow Lane Leads. This data is stored securely within Google's infrastructure and is not accessible to the public.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your information is used exclusively for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or any third-party analytics tools that collect personal data.

Your Rights

You may request that we delete any information you have submitted at any time. To do so, contact us at creighton@bowlaneleads.com.
